My Living Garden — Free Vegetable Garden Planner

My Living Garden is a free vegetable garden planner that helps you map raised beds, calculate seed starting dates, build a planting calendar, track harvests, and plan succession planting — all offline, with no account required.

Plan Your Raised Beds

Draw and label your garden beds on a visual grid. Assign plants to each bed and carry your layout forward season after season.

Seed Starting Calculator

Enter your zip code to look up your local frost date, then get exact weeks-before-frost starting dates for every vegetable and herb in your garden.

Planting Calendar & Task Scheduler

Automatically generate a complete planting calendar from your seed list. Schedule indoor starts, transplant dates, and direct-sow windows in one place.

Succession Planting Gap Finder

Spot gaps in your planting schedule and fill them with succession crops to extend your harvest window throughout the growing season.

7-Day Garden Weather Forecast

Check a hyperlocal weather forecast for your garden location before you plant, water, or harvest.

Harvest Tracking & Activity Log

Log each harvest with weight and notes. Review your activity history to improve your garden plan year over year.

Works Offline — No Account Needed

My Living Garden is a Progressive Web App. Install it on your phone or tablet and use it anywhere in the garden, even without an internet connection.
